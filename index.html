Do you believe?

Mauricio Porcayo known as "Mizio" began his public career in the suburbs of Chicago in 2023, but has been producing and DJing since 2016. "Bet on Yourself" quite literally means to take a chance on yourself. When you love what you do, everything you have learned will amount to the very moment you need it. To Mauricio, it was not until he "bet on himself" that he began seeing his career grow.
